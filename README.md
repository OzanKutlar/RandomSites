## The Sites

### Workout Tracker (`workout_tracker.html`)

A very simple workout tracker for a HIIT manual resistance bike. It counts down 3 minutes while you do your hard mode, then for 2 while you do easy mode. Along with a pause with space.

When there are 5 seconds left on the 3 or 2 minute periods, the app makes ding noises to get your attention from whatever you're doing while doing a resistance bike.
